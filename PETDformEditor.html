Instructions

1. Fill out the form
2. Once you are done hit the "Save" button at the bottom of the form to download it !

Patient: Use the checkboxes under Habits, Sleep, Exercise, Ergonomics and Other to select any options that applies to you. You can also type in any risk factors that come to mind next to checkboxes without any option.

Doctor or Medical staff: Use the textboxes under all Treatment to provide recommended treatment for the section above it. Finally, fill out the Diagnosis area.
