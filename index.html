Welcome to PainCare

PainCare is a website that allows doctors and their patients to fill the Pain Explanation and Treatment Diagram Form by Hillel Finestone. It is an effective way to treat chronic pains by allowing the patient to identify his or her personnal risk factors and doctors to provide treatment for these factors.

On this website, you can also find other forms that your doctor might require you to fill out ahead of doctors appointment under All forms and some help on filling out the Pain Explanation an Treatment under Help
