HELP

*What can we offer ?
Our task is to create an accessible, bilingual user experience which includes questionnaires that can be used by health specialists and patients. These questionnaires will help record additional patient information between visits to help manage long waiting lists and allow patients to be screened by specialists more efficiently and quickly.

*Is the website trustful ?
All information presented at the website has been verified by health professionals and adapted for distribution and downloading experience. All information recorded at the website will be protected by our privacy policy of personal information protection.

*How can I benefit from this product/service ?
PainCare will help you manage your patients more efficiently between visits. It also works as a guide to new health professionals in the field of chronic pain treatment by giving fiable tools and resources to start a clinic or start treating new patients.

*How much money do I need to have this product ?
PainCare is a free website which allows you to download guiding questionnaires which can be implemented at every clinic or hospital in order to treat patients with chronic pain.
